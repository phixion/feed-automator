Privacy Policy feed-automator devvit app

1. General Information

This Reddit application ("feed-automator") is designed to fetch content from publicly available RSS feeds and post them to a specific subreddit. The App operates on the Reddit Devvit platform.

2. Data Collection and Processing

This app can:

  • collect public Reddit information about users.
  • store information it collects.
  • embed a webapp to display rich content.

3. Data Sharing

No user data is shared with third parties. The only external communication is the request sent to the RSS feed providers to retrieve news updates.

4. Hosting

The App is hosted on servers provided by Reddit Inc. (Devvit Platform). Usage is subject to Reddit’s overall Data Directive and Privacy Policy.
